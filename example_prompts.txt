## Tips for Better Results
- Be specific about style, mood, and composition
- Include lighting descriptions (golden hour, studio lighting, etc.)
- Mention quality terms (highly detailed, 4K, professional)
- Use negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements
- Experiment with different guidance scales (7.5-12.0)
- Try various inference steps (30-70 for balance)
